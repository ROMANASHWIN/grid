Rock beach

Rock beach is regarded as one of the most attractive sight-seeing places in Pondicherry. This beach is situated on the busy streets of Pondicherry. In the evening, a lot of activities take place on the beach which makes the place even more happening. It is surrounded by restaurants, art galleries, and the famous French war memorial is just 0.4 km away from the beach. In order to get a sunrise view, many people book a hotel near the rock beach.

Pondy marina

Pondy Marina Beach, located in the charming coastal city of Puducherry (formerly known as Pondicherry), is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the region. The beach is known for its vast expanse of golden sand, tranquil blue waters, and a serene atmosphere that makes it an ideal destination for relaxation and rejuvenation. With a rich history and culture, Puducherry has been a melting pot of various civilizations and cultures, which is reflected in its architecture, cuisine, and way of life. The Pondy Marina Beach, with its scenic beauty, offers a glimpse into the vibrant culture and heritage of Puducherry and is a must-visit for anyone traveling to this enchanting city.

Formerly a French colony, Pondicherry is the capital of Puducherry, one of the seven Union Territories of India. It still houses a slew of colonial buildings, churches and statues, among others. Its systematic town planning makes it popular by the name of ‘Europe of India’. Although a small town, Pondicherry is famously known for its interesting mix of things including a superb list of churches in Pondicherry.
